Down the Stairs

2019

  Diverging from the conventional approach of button inputs in console games, I aimed to create a system where the game could intelligently deduce whether the player intended to say “X” sound or “Y” sound. My concept behind it was what if my “X” sounds different from your “X”? What if my “X” doesn’t sound the same every time? Recognizing the variability in how individuals pronounce and articulate these commands, I incorporated a machine learning library, TensorFlow. Through a training phase, the system captured and adapted to the diverse sound variations associated with each user's specific command, accommodating the nuances of pronunciation and ensuring robust performance even in instances where the same command might sound different across different users or even from the same user over time.
