Down the Stairs

2019

  Diverging from the conventional approach of button inputs in console games, I developed a system that intelligently interprets vocal commands. I used Tensorflow, a machine learning library, to train user-specific models to ensure highly accurate command recognition, creating a more intuitive and immersive gaming experience. This approach not only explores new ways to interact with games but also offers potential accessibility benefits for players with disabilities, opening up gaming to a broader, more inclusive audience.
