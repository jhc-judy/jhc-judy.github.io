Projection Mapping

2019

  This project evolved from earlier work with projection mapping, now incorporating Vive trackers to lower the barrier to entry of project mapping. By streamlining the projection alignment process, the trackers enable the system to function in dynamic environments where objects may be moved without losing visual synchronization. This development opens possibilities for real-time interactive installations, live performances, or exhibitions where physical and digital elements must interact seamlessly. Future applications could include dynamic stage sets, immersive art installations, or even augmented reality experiences in public spaces.
