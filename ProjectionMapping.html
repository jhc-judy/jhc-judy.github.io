Projection Mapping

2019

  This project was an evolvement from a prior endeavor involving projection mapping using points system in Unity. In this iteration, I introduced the integration of Vive tracker to streamline the calibration process and ensure precise image projection even after relocating physical objects. The incorporation of Vive trackers eliminated the necessity for extensive calibrations, providing a seamless and accurate projection experience in dynamic environments.
