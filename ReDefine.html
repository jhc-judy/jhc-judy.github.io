Re:Define

2019

  ReDefine is a web application that leverages Convolutional Neural Network (CNN), algorithms inspired by the human brain's visual cortex, for real-time image manipulation. Users can capture photos on the fly and have them processed to emulate various famous painting styles of their choosing. The transformed images are showcased in the site's gallery tab, providing users with an interactive experience that celebrates the fusion of art and technology.
