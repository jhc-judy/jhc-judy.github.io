Sound Interactive

2017

  Captivated by the idea of translating sound into visual form, I collaborated with a musician to bring this concept to life. My contribution focused on creating visualizations using 3D software like Cinema4D, leveraging its dynamic features to craft visual effects that responded to the music. Through this, we successfully merged the visual and auditory elements into a cohesive, multi-sensory experience, bridging music and 3D digital artistry.
