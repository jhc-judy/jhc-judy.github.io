VR DJ

2018

As a side project during my internship with HTC, I developed a voice-controlled game using Unity, the Vive Headset, and a Speech Engine, enabling users to adjust music frequency and volume through voice commands. The visualization, including virtual equalizers and dynamic color changes, provided immediate feedback and enhanced user engagement, clarity, and accessibility, showcasing the integration of advanced technologies to elevate the virtual interaction experience.
