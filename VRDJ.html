VR DJ

2018

  During my internship with HTC, I developed a virtual interaction project using Unity, the Vive headset, and a Speech Engine, allowing users to adjust music frequency and volume through voice commands. The project incorporated virtual equalizers and dynamic color changes, providing real-time feedback to enhance user engagement, clarity, and accessibility. By integrating these technologies, I aimed to elevate the immersive experience, demonstrating how voice-controlled systems can create more intuitive and engaging virtual environments.
