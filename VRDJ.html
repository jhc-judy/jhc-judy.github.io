VR DJ

2018

As a side project during my internship with HTC, I developed a voice control game, leveraging Unity, the Vive Headset, and a Speech Engine. I created an interactive experience wherein users could manipulate the targeted frequency on the equalizer and adjust the volume of the music through voice commands. This project showcased the integration of cutting-edge technologies to enhance user interaction and control within a virtual environment.
