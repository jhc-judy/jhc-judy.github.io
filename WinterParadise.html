  Winter Paradise is an experimental piece inspired by a tropical escape. The ceramic components within this piece intricately mirror the delicate beauty of coral reefs and the rich ecosystems they host. At the heart of this creation lies the celebration of the harmonious coexistence between nature and technology. As the carousel spins, the light dances upon the ceramic surfaces, casting shadows that capture the dynamic essence of water in motion. This play of light and shadow adds a layer of depth, symbolizing the ever-changing nature of the underwater world. The delicate interplay unfolds between the fragility of nature and the precision of technology. It serves not just as an artistic creation but as a profound narrative, urging us to reflect on our responsibility to preserve the enchanting wonders beneath the waves.
