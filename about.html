JHC_JUDY is a multidisciplinary artist with a passion for blending traditional art forms with cutting-edge technology. She holds a B.S. in Computer Science and Studio Arts with Honors from Stony Brook University, where she also minored in Digital Arts. Her expertise in both art and technology has earned her recognition in various hackathons and award competitions, including the Gabriel Award for Excellence in Painting, SBUHacks-Best Game Dev, Hack@CEWIT-VR Best Dev, and Hackfeat-1st Place. With a background in both traditional art and computer science, Judy's work pushes the boundaries of what is possible in the world of art and technology. She is always looking to improve her skills and expand her creative horizons.
